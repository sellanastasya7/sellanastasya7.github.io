Where to post

JotterPad is perfect for writing novels, poems, song lyrics, and articles. The writing format that you create in JotterPad can be .txt, pdf, markdown, and docx.

Wattpad is a writing application which is an online community for writers and readers.

Storial.co is a writing application for you who want to upload your writing or story in book format. The feature on Storial.co allows users to write the story chapter by chapter.
